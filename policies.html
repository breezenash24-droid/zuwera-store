Shipping Policy

All Zuwera orders are processed within 1–3 business days after your order is confirmed. Orders placed on weekends or US public holidays will begin processing the next business day.

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Please allow up to 24 hours for tracking to update.

Domestic Shipping (United States)

  • Standard Shipping — 5 to 7 business days
  • Free standard shipping on all orders over $100
  • Orders under $100 — flat rate of $6.99

Zuwera currently ships to all 50 US states including Alaska and Hawaii. PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses are accepted for standard shipping.
