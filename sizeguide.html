How to Measure

A
Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
B
Waist
Measure around your natural waistline, where you naturally bend. Keep it snug but not tight.
C
Hips
Measure around the fullest part of your hips and buttocks, keeping the tape parallel.
D
Height
Stand straight against a wall without shoes. Measure from the top of your head to the floor.
E
Head Circumference
Measure around the fullest part of your head, about an inch above your eyebrows.
Tips for Accurate Measurements:

For the most accurate results, measure over your undergarments or light clothing in the morning when you're well-rested.

Use a soft measuring tape and keep it snug but not tight against your body. Avoid pulling or stretching the tape.

Tall & Short Sizing: If you're significantly taller or shorter than average, consider adding or subtracting 2 inches from sleeve and body length recommendations.
