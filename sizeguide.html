How to Measure

A
Chest Width
Measure flat across the chest, 1 inch below the armhole. This is a half-chest measurement.
B
Body Length
Measured from the highest point of the shoulder seam straight down to the finished hem.
D
Shoulder Width
Measured from shoulder seam to shoulder seam across the back of the garment, laid flat.
E
Sleeve Length
Measured from the center back neck, over the shoulder and down to the cuff opening.
G
Bottom Sweep
Measured flat across the bottom hem of the garment. Multiply by 2 for full circumference.
