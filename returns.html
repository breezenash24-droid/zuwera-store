Returns

Free returns & exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

Return Policy
30-day returns Free return shipping All items eligible

We want you to love what you wear. If you are not completely satisfied with your Zuwera purchase, you may return eligible items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

How to Start a Return

Use the form on this page to submit your request and we will send a prepaid return shipping label within 1–2 business days. Pack your item securely and drop it off at any USPS location. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the item and issued to the original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items marked Final Sale at time of purchase
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Gift cards

Exchanges

We process exchanges as a return and repurchase. Submit a return for the original item and place a new order for the size or style you need — this ensures you get the item before stock runs out.

Still Have Questions?

Email us at nasirubreeze@zuwera.store and we will get back to you within one business day.
